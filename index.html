Awareness

  • Don’t take eve teasing lightly

  • While using 2 wheeler be sure to wear helmet at all times (especially at night). Don’t stop for any stranger

  • While travelling at night don’t keep your phone in your hand

  • After getting dropped don’t stay outside to take a call
